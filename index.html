Who we are
 
Seb Trans Services Ltd is a newly formed UK based company and our main activity is commercial containers transportation from Dartford Port. Its owner and manager is a former HGV driver with more than 15 years experience in the transport industry, both in the UK and across the continental Europe. Since launch in 2015 Seb Trans Services Ltd is in a continuous expansion aiming to sustainingly improve the quality of its services. Our employee's experience and the professional customer service we provide assured us a guaranteed place by the side of other more popular companies in the very competitive transport industry. Our goal is to provide a flawless overall experience for all of our customers. As a small but growing company, we benefit from the fact that we can overrule the corporate approach in managing our business which means a very fast problem-solving response time for any issue that day-to-day work may encounter.That combined with a modern approach towards our client's needs makes us stand out of the crowd.
